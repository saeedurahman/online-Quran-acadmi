Our Mission:

We aim to provide quality Quranic and Islamic education online. The main focus is on reciting the Holy Quran with the rules of Tajweed. Tajweed is necessary for reverence and submission in Salah (Namaz). Moreover, Tajweed is one of the etiquettes to read the holy Quran. Online Quran academy considers that the basic knowledge of Islam should reach every Muslim. The “online” aspect of the academy is an extension of that goal.

Welcome to Online Quran Academy!

Quran-Edu is an Online Quran academy. It started working in 2011. And aims to spread Quranic education around the world. The lack of Islamic educational institutes in online space has created a vacuum. It needs to be filled so that everyone can learn Islam and the Quran. We are bringing the light of the Holy Quran to online spaces. Our online Quran teachers are dedicated, qualified, hardworking, and experienced. We take special care of students’ needs. The online Quran academy offers flexible hours. Students can choose the best time possible out of their busy schedules. The institute has made videos of the lessons to help with the homework. The online Quran courses offered are easy and engaging. At our online Quran academy, we believe everyone, regardless of age, gender, or occupation, is entitled to learn the Holy Quran. In this message, we believe with all our hearts. In this, we want others to believe. So, join us and be one of us!

Welcome to Tajweed Ul Quran – Your Ultimate Resource for Perfecting Quranic Recitation:

At Tajweed Ul Quran, we are dedicated to helping you master the art of Quranic recitation with precision and clarity. Our website offers a comprehensive range of resources and tools designed to guide you in perfecting your tajweed (the rules of Quranic pronunciation)

What We Offer about Tajweed:

Interactive Tajweed Lessons: Our expert-led tutorials cover all aspects of tajweed, from basic rules to advanced techniques, ensuring you understand and apply them correctly. Audio and Video Resources: Explore a wide array of audio and video materials that demonstrate proper pronunciation and recitation, making learning engaging and effective.

Welcome to Hefzul Ul Quran – Your Pathway to Memorizing the Quran

At Hefzul Ul Quran, we are committed to supporting and guiding you on your journey to memorizing the Holy Quran. Our platform is designed to provide you with the tools, resources, and support necessary to achieve your memorization goals with ease and confidence

What We Offer:

Structured Memorization Programs: Our comprehensive programs are tailored to cater to all levels, whether you’re starting from scratch or looking to refine your memorization. We provide a step-by-step approach that makes the process manageable and effective. Expert Guidance: Learn from experienced teachers and scholars who offer personalized coaching and strategies to help you overcome challenges and stay motivated throughout your memorization journey. Interactive Tools: Utilize our range of interactive tools, including memorization aids, quizzes, and progress trackers, to keep you engaged and on track.
